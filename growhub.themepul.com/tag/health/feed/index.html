Holistic user-centric approach:

Recognize the importance of mental health. Provide access to counseling services, create a stigma-free environment, and promote mental health awareness campaigns. Offer flexibility in work arrangements, including remote work opportunities. This not only enhances work-life balance but also accommodates diverse needs and preferences.

Consider flexible work hours or compressed workweeks. Allowing employees to choose schedules that align with their peak productivity times fosters a sense of autonomy. Invest in ergonomic furniture and encourage employees to maintain proper posture. Ergonomically designed workspaces contribute to physical well-being and can prevent chronic health issues.

Optimize workspace design to incorporate natural light and green spaces. Exposure to natural elements positively influences mood, reduces stress, and enhances overall well-being.
