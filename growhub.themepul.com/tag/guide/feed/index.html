Holistic user-centric approach:

Implement value stream mapping to visualize and analyze the entire workflow. This identifies areas of waste and inefficiency, laying the foundation for targeted improvements. Organize Kaizen events, short-term improvement projects focused on specific areas. These events bring cross-functional teams together to rapidly implement changes and achieve quick wins.

Adopt the DMAIC framework from Six Sigma for problem-solving and process improvement. This structured approach ensures systematic and data-driven decision-making. Implement SPC techniques to monitor and control processes. Statistical tools help identify variations, allowing for timely adjustments and continuous optimization.
